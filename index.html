--- layout: home title: The evolution of the image of Tesla subtitle: Analysis of the image of Tesla through quotes, taken at the release of several of their main products --- This story aims to analyse how Tesla and its well-known CEO Elon Musk have become respectively one of the most important vehicle manufacturers and influential person in the world. Tesla was founded in July 2003 as Tesla Motors, the name being in honor of Nikola Tesla. Elon Musk became the largest shareholder of the company when he made an investment of 6.5 million USD in February 2004. He has served as CEO of the company since 2008. We wish to study the evolution of the global perception of Tesla by analyzing quotes taken from the dataset *Quotebank*, through - for now - two cases studies : - The release of the Tesla Model 3, which is today the all-time best-selling plug-in electric car worldwide, and the first electric car to sell one million units globally - The release of the Model Y or the cybertruck.
